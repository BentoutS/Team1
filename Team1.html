Team Overview

We pioneer cutting-edge mathematical models for complex biological systems, bridging theory and real-world applications in epidemiology, ecology, and dynamical systems.

Research Focus

  • Differential equations (ODE/PDE) & spectral theory
  • Mathematical epidemiology (SIR/SIS models)
  • Ecological modeling & stability analysis
  • Interdisciplinary collaborations with computational biology
