Advanced Biomathematics Innovation (ABI)

Bridging Mathematical Theory and Biological Complexity

Team Overview

We are an interdisciplinary research team developing advanced mathematical models to solve complex problems in epidemiology, ecology, and dynamical systems. Our work combines rigorous theoretical analysis with practical applications to address real-world challenges.

Core Research Themes

Dynamical Systems in Biology

Developing ODE/PDE frameworks for biological processes with applications to disease modeling and population dynamics.

Fractional Calculus Applications

Exploring non-local operators for modeling memory effects in ecological and epidemiological systems.

Stability & Control Theory

Lyapunov methods and spectral analysis for complex biological networks.
